The Plant Phenology Ontology: Term Definitions and Documentation

The PPO can be logically divided into two sets of classes: first, classes for phenological stages, and second, classes for phenological traits. These classes work together, along with supporting classes from other key ontologies, to describe the phenology of a particular plant or plant community at some place and time.
